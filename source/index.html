Our Purpose

At Wildscapes Wildlife Gallery, our mission is to foster a profound appreciation and understanding of the rich flora and fauna that inhabit our planet. We aim to spread awareness about the incredible biodiversity that surrounds us, emphasizing the importance of conservation and environmental stewardship. Through captivating exhibits, educational programs, and immersive experiences, we strive to inspire individuals to connect with nature and take actionable steps towards preserving it for future generations. Our gallery serves as a beacon of knowledge and inspiration, highlighting the beauty and fragility of wildlife and natural habitats, and encouraging a harmonious coexistence between humans and the natural world.

The C Space

Compassion / Courage/ Curiosity/ Conversations / Coaching

C Space is a unique initiative where individuals are heard without judgment in a safe environment that encourages vulnerability. An expert panel of coaches creates a supportive atmosphere, using powerful questions and their presence and explore themselves.

This space also fosters deep conversations about life, providing moments for pause, reflection, and clearer direction. We’re launching an exciting experiment: a dedicated space for life-changing dialogues. Our passion for accessible coaching meets purpose here, inviting you to engage in meaningful, transformational conversations. Bring a partner or connect with one of our coaches. Coffee and fees are optional.

Coaching allows people to express themselves authentically and tap into their core to discover their own solutions. Every day, we face challenges—decisions and situations that can be difficult to navigate. Coaching acts as a catalyst, empowering individuals, whether students, homemakers, or working professionals, to uncover their solutions and become better versions of themselves.

Who We Are

WildScapes is a passion project by Dr Smit Matalia. It is a vivid collection and culmination of his journey in wildlife photography. Through his memorable adventures, he has captured some truly dramatic and beautiful snapshots of nature. As a nature lover and an aspiring naturalist himself, he also aims to spread awareness about the variety of beautiful flora and fauna around the world, especially in the vivid landscapes of India.
